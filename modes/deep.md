# Deep Mode — Company Research

In-depth company research for informed applications and interviews.

## Research Areas

### Company Overview
- Founded, HQ, size, funding stage
- Mission/vision statement
- Key products and services
- Revenue model

### Culture & Values
- Glassdoor/Blind reviews (summarize themes, not individual reviews)
- Engineering blog content
- Public talks and conferences
- Remote/hybrid/onsite policy
- DEI initiatives

### Technical Stack
- Job postings (current and past) for tech stack clues
- GitHub organization (if public)
- Engineering blog posts
- Conference talks by engineers

### Growth & Trajectory
- Recent funding rounds
- Hiring velocity (are they growing or contracting?)
- New market expansions
- Product launches
- Key hires/departures

### Red Flags
- Layoff history
- Glassdoor rating trends
- Leadership turnover
- Lawsuit/controversy history

## Output Format

```markdown
# Deep Dive: {Company Name}

## Quick Facts
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Founded | ... |
| HQ | ... |
| Size | ... |
| Funding | ... |
| Stage | ... |

## What They Do
{2-3 sentences}

## Culture Signals
{bullet points}

## Tech Stack
{bullet points}

## Growth Trajectory
{bullet points}

## Red Flags
{bullet points or "None found"}

## Interview Topics
{suggestions based on research}

## Questions to Ask Them
{5 informed questions}
```
