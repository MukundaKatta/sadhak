# Outreach Mode — LinkedIn & Email

Generate personalized outreach messages for networking and warm introductions.

## Message Types

### Connection Request (300 char limit)
Short, specific, no ask. Just establish relevance.
> "Hi {name}, I saw {company} is hiring for {role}. I've been working on {relevant thing} — would love to connect and learn more about the team."

### Follow-up Message (after connection)
Slightly longer, one clear ask.
- Reference something specific about their work
- Share one relevant proof point
- Ask one question (not "can I have a job")

### InMail (if premium)
Subject + body. More formal, still concise.
- Subject: specific and intriguing, not generic
- Body: 3-4 sentences max

### Email (cold outreach)
For when you have a direct email.
- Subject line: `{Role} @ {Company} — {One-liner about you}`
- Body: 4-5 sentences max
- Always include: why you're reaching out, one proof point, one ask

## Rules

- NEVER be desperate or transactional
- NEVER ask for a referral in the first message
- ALWAYS personalize (reference their work, posts, company news)
- ALWAYS have one clear, easy-to-answer ask
- Keep it SHORT — respect their time
- Match the language of their profile/company

## Context Sources

1. Evaluation report (if exists) for company context
2. Profile.yml for candidate positioning
3. WebSearch for the person's recent activity/posts
